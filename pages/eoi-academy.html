Requirements for performance stream

AMEB Grade 7 (or equivalent) or above in any woodwind, brass or string instrument. Applicants are required to submit a CV and list of relevant solo and/or ensemble repertoire that they have previously performed, as well as up to 3 pieces that they wish to study at the Academy.

No age restrictions.

Requirements for composition stream

Applications are assessed by CV and composition portfolio on a case-by-case basis. Participants must have some experience in the composition of new and/or experimental (art) music. This may include notated and/or electronic music.

No age restrictions.
