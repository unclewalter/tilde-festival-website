For the 2017 festival, we are exhibiting fixed media works in a new sound installation entited "strange fruit" featuring 12 individually addressible speakers hung at staggered heights from an aluminium tree. For assessment purposes, submissions for this call will be given in stereo (file format requirments below). If your work is accepted into the festival, we will request a multi-channel version of the work up to 12 channels for presentation in the installation.

Maximum duration: fifteen minutes

Submission requirements: Please submit your work as an uncompressed 16-bit 44.1kHz wav or aiff file.

Deadline: 31st of December 2016

If you have any further questions about this particular call, please email the Tilde New Music production coordator, Kevan Atkins at kevan@kevanatkins.com

This information will be used for correspondance with the Tilde team and will not be disseminated to others.
